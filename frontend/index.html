Our Priority-Based Scheduling

We use an advanced greedy algorithm that prioritizes appointments based on medical urgency, ensuring those who need care most receive it promptly.

How Our Priority System Works

Priority Level Description Examples Scheduling Weight
Emergency Life-threatening conditions requiring immediate attention Severe chest pain, major trauma, severe bleeding Highest priority
High Urgent medical issues requiring prompt attention High fever, minor fractures, severe pain Second priority
Normal Standard medical appointments Follow-ups, minor illness, chronic condition management Third priority
Low Non-urgent medical needs General checkups, prescription refills Lowest priority

Greedy Scheduling Algorithm

Our system uses a greedy algorithm that:

  • Sorts patients by priority level (Emergency > High > Normal > Low)
  • Within the same priority, considers registration time (first-come-first-served)
  • Matches patients with appropriate doctors based on specialization
  • Optimizes time slot allocation to minimize wait times
  • Automatically reschedules when emergency cases arise
